Make it deedle

There is no apps that would allow people to share their doings. Deedle aims to change that. The idea is to help people share and show things they have done.

Do it

Imagine you just saved a cat or planted a tree, take a picture and write a short story about your deed. Here you can check what people think about your deed, if they like it or not. It will be immediately available in our application public timeline.

Public Timeline

Public timeline allows you to monitor the deeds that people make in the world.

No Passwords

No more boring registration. We already know about you what we need.

Evaluation

With evaluation system you can always know what others think about your actions.

Map View

Turn on map view and you will find out what people do in your city or country.

Shortly About Us

Deedle created by three students from Lviv, Ukraine.

First, the idea came to Bogdan Tsap, when he knew about GADC from GDG Lviv. He shared the idea with Dmytro Danylyk. Soon, Eugene Shykiriavyi, the third member, have joined the team.

Deedle uses redistributed storage from users Google Drive accounts and other services like App Engine, Maps, OAuth and more.
